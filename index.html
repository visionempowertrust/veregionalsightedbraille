Teacher notes

What this prototype does

Presents a complete public-facing course shell, usable chart, random recognition practice, and a simple Kannada braille exploration tool.

What to verify

The starter data follows publicly described Kannada/Bharati Braille conventions and should be reviewed by a qualified Kannada Braille educator before production use.

Source basis

Structure inspired by Hadley's sight-reading workshop page. Braille conventions are based on public Kannada and Bharati Braille references.
